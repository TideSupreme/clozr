The escrow officer IS the single point of failure

2023: First American — $1M wire-fraud loss from a single compromised account.

The Solution

We deleted the escrow officer — replaced by a Tide split key

A split key governs both sides: buyer's funds unlock ONLY when seller's deed is signed, and vice versa. Two keys fragmented across Tide's ORK network, bound to one quorum condition: both parties confirm, or nothing moves.

Zero Custodial Risk
